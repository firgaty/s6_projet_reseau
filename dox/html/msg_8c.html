Generates a Hello TLV.

A SHORT hello of 8 bytes is to be sent to a potential pair of which we don't know the ID. The LONG hello of 16 bytes will be sent back with the destination ID included.

Parameters
typeCan be SHORT_HELLO or LONG_HELLO.
source_id
dest_idTo be specified if type is LONG_HELLO, else it will be ignored.
